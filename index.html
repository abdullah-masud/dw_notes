Final Exam Structure — Know This

The final exam has 5 questions, all based on a single scenario. Q1 & Q2: Data Warehouse (40 marks) · Q3: Metadata + Association Rule Mining (20 marks) · Q4 & Q5: Graph Database (40 marks). Closed book — you may bring one double-sided A4 page.
